About Futsal


Futsal is a variant of soccer played (mainly) indoors on a hard court slightly larger than a basketball court,
with two teams of five players each, including a goalkeeper.
It uses a smaller, heavier, low-bounce ball and is recognized by FIFA as the official indoor soccer game.
